Ghost Economics

Fair fees, sustainable incentives, and a clear path to decentralization through treasury decay.

TL;DR
No new tokens. No inflation. All rewards come from Bitcoin itself. Treasury caps at 21 BTC then decays to zero over 5 years — the system ossifies.

Pool Fee Model

Ghost charges a fixed 1% fee on block subsidy only. Transaction fees always go 100% to the winning miner.

5-4-3-2-1 Node Reward System

Nodes earn 0-15 shares from the Node Reward Pool based on their behavior. More shares = larger portion of the Node Reward Pool. 95% uptime required as gatekeeper.

Treasury & Decay Model

Once the Treasury reaches 21 BTC, a 5-year decay begins. Ghost becomes fully decentralized with zero governance.
