Most Compatible

Stratum V1 (SV1)

Works with all ASIC miners (Antminer, Whatsminer, Avalon, BitAxe) and software miners (CGMiner, BFGMiner).

Port: 3333
High Performance

Stratum V2 (SV2)

Next-gen protocol with encryption and reduced bandwidth. Requires SV2-compatible firmware or translator.

Port: 34255 (via SRI translator)

Why Mine with Ghost?

Benefits over traditional centralized pools.

💰
100% TX Fees to Winner
100% TX Fees to the Node that hosts the winning miner. So run your own Node and when you find a block, you keep ALL the transaction fees. No sharing, no pool cut on fees.
🔒
Censorship Resistant
Decentralized infrastructure means no single point of censorship. Your mining is sovereign.
👻
Template Privacy
Ghost Mode nodes build templates privately. No one sees your block until it's broadcast.
⚡
Low Latency
Connect to your nearest Ghost node. ZeroMQ-based protocol minimizes share latency.
📊
Transparent Payouts
BFT consensus ensures all payouts are verified by the network. No trust required.
🌐
No Account Required
Just point your miner and start hashing. Your Bitcoin address is your identity.
