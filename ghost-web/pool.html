In line to get paid.

The ledger. Every share you submit accumulates on your unpaid balance. When a block is found the top 1000 miners by unpaid work (filtered to those above the 546-sat dust threshold) get included in the coinbase, proportional to their share. Everyone else keeps their ledger intact and carries it forward to compete for the next block. Unpaid shares expire only if a miner goes dark for 7 days.
