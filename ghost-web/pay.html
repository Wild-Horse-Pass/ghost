how it works

Private payments that settle.

Ghost Pay is an L2 on top of Bitcoin Ghost. Payments are confirmed instantly against shielded notes, mixed through Wraith when you want privacy, and settled on-chain every epoch. No custodian, no API keys, no chargebacks.

  • Instant confirmation
    Payments accept in milliseconds against an L2 commitment. On-chain settlement happens in the background on a fixed cadence.
  • Shielded notes
    Fixed-denomination Pedersen commitments. Amounts are hidden on-chain; spenders prove ownership with a ZK circuit, never exposing balances.
  • Wraith mixing
    Coordinator-free rounds where participants submit shielded inputs and receive unlinkable outputs. Break the chain on your own schedule.
  • Self-custody, always
    Keys live on your device. The pool never holds user funds — it only settles commitments you signed.
  • Fees go to nodes
    Same decay schedule as L1: pre-21-BTC, fees split 50/50 between treasury and node reward pool. Post-decay, 100% to nodes.
  • No asset issuance
    Ghost Pay moves Bitcoin, not tokens. L2 notes are one-to-one-backed by L1 sats locked in a timelocked vault. Unshield any time.
