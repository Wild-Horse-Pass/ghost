Getting Started

To start mining with Bitcoin Ghost:

  1. Install a Stratum-compatible mining software
  2. Configure your miner:
    • Pool: stratum+tcp://pool.bitcoinghost.org:3333
    • Worker: Your Bitcoin address
    • Password: x
  3. Start mining and check your stats here
