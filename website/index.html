Why Bitcoin Ghost?

⚡

Stratum V2 Native

Full Stratum V2 support with job negotiation, reducing bandwidth and increasing efficiency. SV1 translation layer for legacy miners.

🌐

Decentralized Nodes

Multiple independent nodes across regions. Smart routing connects you to the best node for your location automatically.

🔒

Non-Custodial

Your keys, your coins. Payouts go directly to your address. No accounts required, just point your miner and earn.

📈

Transparent

Open source pool software. Real-time stats and complete payout history. 1% pool fee goes to development.

💻

Easy Setup

One-line installer, Docker support, or binary downloads. Get mining in minutes with sensible defaults.

🛠

Operator Friendly

Run your own node to serve your miners. Local dashboard, swarm management, and full API access.

Quick Start

curl -sSL https://bitcoinghost.org/install.sh | bash

Or configure your existing miner:

Pool: stratum+tcp://pool.bitcoinghost.org:3333
Worker: <your_btc_address>
Password: x
